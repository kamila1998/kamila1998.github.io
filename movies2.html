Sherlock Holmes

Genre: Detective fiction short stories.

Release data: 2014y.

Sherlock depicts "consulting detective" Sherlock Holmes (Benedict Cumberbatch) solving various mysteries in modern-day London. Holmes is assisted by his flatmate and friend, Dr John Watson (Martin Freeman), who has returned from military service in Afghanistan with the Royal Army Medical Corps. Although Metropolitan Police Service Detective Inspector Greg Lestrade (Rupert Graves) and others are at first suspicious of Holmes, over time his exceptional intellect and bold powers of observation persuade them of his value. In part through Watson's blog documenting their adventures, Holmes becomes a reluctant celebrity with the press reporting on his cases and eccentric personal life. Both ordinary people and the British government ask for his help. >

You are my home

Genre: Drama, Romance

Release data: Nov 30, 2012y.

Turkish writer/director Ozcan Deniz stars in an intense romantic drama. Following a painful separation, broken-hearted Leyla (Fahriye Evcen) decides to return home to recuperate from the trauma. But there’s an additional challenge. She must also repair the broken bonds with her strict authoritarian father, Selim (Sait Gunay). Her path soon crosses with that of Iskender (Ozcan Deniz). An emotionally damaged construction worker who was raised in an orphanage, he lives alone and has never had a home to call his own. The mutual attraction is apparent from their first exchanged glance, but love takes longer to blossom. Unfortunately, the couple couldn’t be more different. She’s been scarred by her experiences, while his life has been characterised by loneliness. He also struggles to imagine life as a husband. On these delicate foundations, they start to build a relationship. But there’s trouble on the horizon and their happiness could prove to be short-lived >
